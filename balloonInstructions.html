Game #1 Instructions

Estimated 2 minutes.
Requires a mouse.
The goal of this game is to earn as much money as possible. You will be presented with 40 deflated balloons, one at a time.

For each balloon you earn 0.05 per pump. To permanently collect the coins, click “Collect money”.

Each balloon has a explosion point. If the balloon explodes before you click “Collect money”, you will lose all potential money.
